Chocolate Chip Pancakes

Pro tip: “Eat while hot so the chocolate is all gooey!”

READY IN: 15 minutes
YIELD: 12 pancakes
SERVES: 12

Ingredients:

  • 11⁄4 cups flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 tablespoons melted butter
  • 3⁄4 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1⁄3 cup chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Preheat fry pan (griddle or skillet).
  2. Combine flour, sugar, cinnamon, baking powder and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Mix together wet ingredients and beat into dry mixture until smooth.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Pour or spoon batter into fry pan in desired quantity.
  6. Flip when top begins to bubble, then cook a minute more.
